## Description

Expat Weather is the weather app built for people living abroad. Whether you've moved across the world or just across a border, stay connected to the weather back home while keeping track of conditions where you are now.

Unlike other weather apps, Expat Weather shows both Celsius and Fahrenheit simultaneously — no more mental math. Set your home city and compare conditions side-by-side with wherever you are today.

FREE FEATURES:
- Real-time weather for any city worldwide
- Dual temperature display (°C and °F shown together)
- 3-hour forecast with weather icons
- 5-day forecast with temperature range bars
- Detailed conditions: humidity, wind, pressure, visibility, cloud cover
- Save up to 2 cities
- Sunrise and sunset countdown with live arc tracker
- Animated weather backgrounds (rain, snow, clouds, lightning, fog, stars)
- Dark and light mode with auto-switching
- Pull-to-refresh for instant updates
- Beautiful glassmorphic design

EXPAT WEATHER PRO:
- True 24-hour forecast (hourly, not 3-hour blocks)
- Home city comparison card (side-by-side weather)
- Real-time severe weather alerts
- Unlimited saved cities
- Support independent development

DESIGNED FOR EXPATS:
- Onboarding asks "Where's home?" and "Where are you now?"
- Quickly switch between cities you care about
- Set any city as your home location
- See how the weather compares back home at a glance

Powered by OpenWeather. Your location data stays on your device — we never collect or share personal information.
